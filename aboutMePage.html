About Rebekah

Rebekah Lubnick is a 16-year-old girl living on Long Island. This year, she is attending her last year of Syosset High School. Her interests include theatre, singing, travel and computer programming. This summer, she traveled to Thailand with Global Leadership Adventures to volunteer and teach young Thai children to read. She also studied at Adobe with the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program learning how to code.
